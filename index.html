What is LTC?
LTC is a annual convention where Key Clubbers from all over the New York District gather to celebrate the elapsing service year. At the conference, there is an awards ceremony to recognize individual members and clubs for their outstanding performance in the past service year, workshops to educate the newly elected offciers who will lead the next service year, and more!
