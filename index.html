69th Annual Leadership Training Conference

What's LTC?


Leadership Training Conference, or LTC, is a yearly gathering of key clubbers, from all over New York, to celebrate the closing of the service year and the start of a new one. This year's masquerade-themed training conference will be on March 31st to April 2nd and located at the Desmond Hotel.

Over the weekend, key clubbers will be attending workshops, participating in group activities, voting for their next executive board, entering in contests and receiving recognition of what they or their club has accomplished throughout the service year!
