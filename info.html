Conference Staff

Conference Staff Registration

The Conference Staff works to ensure that all aspects of the New York District Key Club Leadership Training Conference occur as seamlessly as possible. Staff members will be responsible for assisting with making the Conference a fun-filled, successful and safe event for all participants. Responsibilities will include, but are not limited to: manning the security desk; checking workshop rooms to ensure all materials are present and working; assisting with hall patrols; and assisting in the smooth transition of participants between events. You must be able to arrive at The Desmond Hotel on Thursday, March 30, 2017.


Any Key Clubber not on the New York District Board, in good standing with Key Club International and the New York District, may apply to be a member of the Conference Staff.


In order to be a Conference Staff Member Member you MUST:
Complete the application online and have one (1) letter of recommendation.
Be able to be available during the entire conference to complete various tasks.
Reside in a room with other Conference Staff members.
Provide their own transportation from the conference.
Conference Staff Members will get a discounted LTC Registration rate of $100.
In addition, transportation on Thursday will be arranged by the New York District Key Club; however, Conference Staff will return home with their clubs.


The 2017 Conference Staff Application will be available in November 2016.

Election Forms
Executive Officer Agreement
The Executive Officer Agreement is a document that consist of information on running for an Executive Position: Governor, Secretary, and Treasurer. The document gives a brief description of each executive position and what role does each position plays in the New York District. Also, the Executive Officer Agreement contains a form that must be filled out by each candidate and the form must be mailed no later than February 1st to the District Administrator, John Goldstein. A copy to the Executive Officer Agreement can be found here or below.

Endorsement for International Office
The Endorsement for International Office is a document that consist of information on running for an International Position: International Trustee, Vice President and President. The document gives a desciption of each international position and what role does each position plays in Key Club International. Also, the Endorsement for International has an agreement that must be filled out by each international candidate. A copy to the Endorsement for International Office can be found here or below.

Election Packet
The Election Packet covers information regarding seeking office for the 2017-2018 Key Club year. This packet covers procedures, rules, speeches, paperwork, responsibilities, and more. A copy of the Elections Packet can be found here or below.

Medical Release Form
The Medical Release Form must be completed and held onto by all attendees of the Leadership Training Conference. This form helps identify and helps us ensure the safety of the attendee. A copy of the Medical Release Form can be found here or below.
